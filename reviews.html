The Hobbit

The Hobbit is set in Middle-earth and follows home-loving Bilbo Baggins, the hobbit of the title, who joins the wizard Gandalf and the thirteen dwarves of Thorin's Company, on a quest to reclaim the dwarves' home and treasure from the dragon Smaug.It was a great read.

Animal Farm

"Animal Farm" by George Orwell is a chilling allegory about the dangers of power, using a farm animal rebellion to expose how revolutionary ideals can be corrupted by manipulative leaders, ultimately leaving the oppressed worse off than before, serving as a powerful warning against totalitarian regimes.

Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass

Frederick Douglass, born into slavery in Maryland, endured a brutal life on a plantation, but through his determination, secretly learned to read and write, ultimately escaping to freedom in the North where he became a powerful abolitionist speaker, using his autobiography "Narrative of the Life of Frederick Douglass" to expose the horrors of slavery and advocate for equality.
